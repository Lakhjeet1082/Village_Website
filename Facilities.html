Educational Facilities

Pandit Rajendra Prasad Smarak Trust College

PRPS College of Pharmacy has been established under Pandit Rajendra Prasad Smarak Trust. It is situated on a prominent location at Pooranpur – Pilibhit highway and surrounded by Aravali Hills. The Institute offers four years B.Pharm. degree and two years D. Pharma diploma course which is duly approved by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi (PCI), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, Affiliated to Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (formerly UPTU) Lucknow, U.P. and Board of Technical Education, Lucknow, U.P.

Akal Academy Kajri School

Akal Academy, Kajari Niranjanpur Puranpur, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from I to XII. It is an English Medium school. It is a Society and is part of Akal Academy and is managed by The Kalgidhar Society, Baru Sahib Akal Academy, Kajari Niranjanpur, Puranpur is one of the best Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) schools in Kajari Niranjanpur area of Puranpur and ranks among the top schools in Puranpur.
