About kajri Niranjanpur

Kajari Niranjanpur is a Village in Puranpur Block in Pilibhit District of Uttar Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Bareilly Division . It is located 61 KM towards East from District head quarters Pilibhit. 23 KM from Puranpur. 211 KM from State capital Lucknow Kajari Niranjanpur Pin code is 230403 and postal head office is Madhoganj (Pratapgarh). Piparia Majhara ( 5 KM ) , Gaura ( 6 KM ) , Haripur T.chandpur ( 6 KM ) , Rampur M. Kon ( 6 KM ) , Basupur ( 7 KM ) are the nearby Villages to Kajari Niranjanpur. Kajari Niranjanpur is surrounded by Banda Block towards west , Puranpur Block towards North , Bilsanda Block towards west , Powayan Block towards South . Puranpur , Powayan , Palia Kalan , Pilibhit are the near by Cities to Kajari Niranjanpur.
